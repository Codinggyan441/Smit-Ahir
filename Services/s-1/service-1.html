One Website, Every Device.

The "Mobile-First" Strategy

In a world where 60% of web traffic is mobile, I build from the smallest screen up. This ensures your site is fast, lightweight, and perfectly readable on smartphones before scaling beautifully to 4K monitors.

Fluid Grid Systems

I use CSS Grid and Flexbox to create layouts that flow like water, filling the available space without ever breaking or causing horizontal scrolls.

Adaptive Typography

Text size dynamically adjusts based on the screen width, ensuring perfect legibility whether on a laptop or a handheld device.

Cross-Browser Testing

Your site is rigorously tested on Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge to ensure a bug-free experience for 100% of your audience.
